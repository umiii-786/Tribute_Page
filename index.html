About 'Khan-Sahb'

FullName: Imran Ahmed Khan Niazi.
FatherName:Ikramullah Khan Niazi
Birth:October 5, 1952,
Place:Lahore
Profession:Imran Khan is a prominent Pakistani politician, former international cricketer, and the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan.

His life and career span several significant phases:

Criciter

Khan's cricket career began in earnest in the early 1970s. He played for Pakistan's national team from 1971 to 1992, captaining the team from 1982 to 1992.

Philanthropy

As a philanthropist, his contributions to healthcare and education have had a lasting impact on Pakistan.

Politician

Imran Khan founded PTI in 1996, aiming to bring about social, economic, and political reforms in Pakistan.
